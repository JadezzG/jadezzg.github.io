In Ragnarok X: Next Generation, gifting NPCs is more than just a friendly gesture. As you increase your intimacy with these characters, you'll unlock valuable rewards and bonuses. This guide covers all you need to know about the NPC favor system - from unlocking gift options to each NPC's favorite items and the rewards they offer.

NPC Gift Basics

In Ragnarok X: Next Generation, you can build relationships with NPCs by giving them gifts. This system rewards players who take the time to increase their intimacy with key characters throughout the game world.

How to Check NPC Gifting Progress

You can check your NPC Gifting progress by going to your Academy Handbook, then open the NPCs tab.

From here, you'll find all of the list and the amount of progress you have with the NPCs. You can also claim here all of the rewards you get from the NPC.

How to Unlock NPC Gifting

You need to complete specific condition for you to gift the NPC. Once you have completed the condition, you'll get a notification you have unlocked the favor to the NPC. From here, the Gift icon will appear every time you talk to the NPC.

Then click Gift button at the message box near the NPC's avatar.

Then you can gift any item to the NPC, the progress will significantly increase when you give their favorite gift.

Gifting Mechanics

Important things to know about the gifting system:

  • You can give any item to any NPC, but each NPC has specific favorite gifts that provide increased intimacy
  • Favorite gifts generally give +1.66% intimacy versus the standard +0.33%
  • There's a daily limit to how many gifts you can give each NPC
  • Intimacy progress is tracked from 0% to 100%
  • Most NPCs provide rewards at 20%, 50%, and 100% intimacy levels
  • Common rewards include Adventure EXP bonuses, but many NPCs offer special items or buffs

Kingdom Pass Quest Tips

Attention to everyone, since the number of NPCs you can gift is limited, and this was recently added as part of the daily quest for Kingdom Pass. Eventually, you will complete all of the available NPC gifting and reached the 100% favor. When this happens, there will no longer be any NPCs to gift.

Tip: To avoid this scenario, you should select a single NPC from the list dedicated for the Kingdom Pass quest and send him/her the gift he/she dislikes. This way, the favor level will remain below 100%, allowing you to still gift every day for the daily KP quest.

NPC Enchantment Buff Weekly Quest

For all unlocked NPC enchantment favor, here are the two buffs you can get from the Weekly Favor Quests:

  • Magic Perception: Grants a chance of conserving enchantment items when enchanting. (Disappears after taking effect 3 times.)
  • Magic Surge: Grants a slightly higher chance of leveling up equipment when enchanting. (Disappears after taking effect 3 times.)

Aside from the enchantment buff, you also need to gather items to complete the weekly quest.

Enchantment NPCs

Enchantment NPCs are among the most important characters to build intimacy with, as they provide valuable buffs for the enchantment process. These NPCs are found in every major city and specialize in different equipment types.

Updates

[UPDATE as of 02/18/2022] – Ragnarok X Next Generation finally releases the level 15 caps for Enchantment. Together with that, favor gift for every enchantment NPC in all cities are now giftable, you can now unlock their favors by enchanting 50 times.

Common Rewards

All NPC listed has a common favor rewards when you reach certain intimacy levels:

  • 20%: Adventure Experience +1
  • 50%: Adventure Experience +2
  • 100%: Adventure Experience +5
  • 100%: Unlocks next Enchantment Level cap (Lv. 15) and Weekly Quests

Unlocking Enchantment NPC Favor

For all enchantment NPCs, you need to enchant equipment 50 times to unlock the ability to gift them. This count is specific to each city, so you'll need to enchant in each location separately to unlock all NPCs.

Common Favorite Gifts

While each enchantment NPC has their specific favorite items, there are patterns in what they prefer:

  • Weapon Enchanters: Old Weapons (+1.66%), Swordfish (+1.66%), Fishing Materials (+1.0%), Instance Extra Drop (Gift) (+1.0%), NPC/COC Materials (+0.33%)
  • Armor Enchanters: Butterfly Cowrie (+1.66%), Crystal Rose (+1.66%), Fishing/Gardening Materials (+1.0%), Bait/NPC/COC Materials (+0.33%)
  • Accessory Enchanters: Leaf Bookmark (+1.66%), Arcane Flower (+1.66%), Gardening Extra Drop/Materials (+1.0%), NPC/COC/Bait Materials (+0.33%)

Gift Efficiency Tip: Most enchantment NPCs give +1.66% intimacy for their favorite items versus just +0.33% for regular items. This means gifting favorites is 5 times more efficient!

Making the Most of NPC Favor

Building intimacy with NPCs can significantly enhance your Ragnarok X: Next Generation experience. Here are some final tips:

  • Prioritize Enchantment NPCs - Their rewards have the most direct impact on your character's progression
  • Save high-value gifts for NPCs that provide the most valuable rewards
  • Remember Kingdom Pass quests - Keep at least one NPC below 100% intimacy
  • Check your Academy Handbook regularly to track your progress and claim rewards

This guide is regularly updated with the latest information. Last updated: July 2023.

Note: NPC favorite gifts and rewards may change with game updates. If you notice any discrepancies or have additional information to share, please let us know!

NPC Favor Tips

  • Focus on enchantment NPCs first for maximum benefits
  • Use favorite gifts for 5x more intimacy points
  • Select one NPC and give gifts they dislike to keep below 100% for Kingdom Pass quests
  • All enchantment NPCs require 50 enchants to unlock
  • Complete weekly quests for Magic Perception and Magic Surge buffs
